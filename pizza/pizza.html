Pizza

This is my pizza

Description

Cut of pizza made on grill

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • mozzarella
  • yeast

Steps

  1. Mix dough, salt, yeast and olive oil
  2. Let it sit for 30min
  3. Work the dough and stretch it in preferred shape
  4. Pour tomato sauce over dough
  5. Place mozzarella in circles
  6. Put it on grill or in oven
